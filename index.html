Measuring America:
People, Places, and Economy

Partner with the U.S. Census Bureau to help America work better through data

Make the most of Census Bureau data.

The Census Bureau is America's leading source of quality data about the nation's people, places, and economy. Whether you’re a business, school, or nonprofit, we’ll work with you to find new and innovative ways to partner.

From corporations to policymakers, foundations and nonprofits to developers — we work across sectors and industries.

Why partner with the U.S. Census Bureau

Drive participation

Ensure your constituents, customers, and stakeholders are represented in vital Census Bureau household and business surveys.

Make our data work for you

Create valuable new tools that improve business, policy, and American lives.

Partner-driven workshops help increase data use and awareness and strengthen participation in Census Bureau surveys and programs.
